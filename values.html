Values Join

A mindset tool for evaluating decisions: does this opportunity actually join with what matters to you, with a good use of time, and with something you can genuinely own — work on and manage — rather than something that depends on other people or circumstances? Modeled loosely on a SQL join: an opportunity only "matches" if it lines up on all three keys, not just one.

The index

Whatever's happening — an environment, a cause, an event in your life — gets joined against this index and labeled as one of the three.

⚫ Fear

  • Fear should be low
  • Stupidity is low
  • Worry
  • Paranoia needs to be understood

🟠 Adamance

  • Adamance exists in positive and negative ways
  • Callousness, pride, loyalty, purpose
  • Trust
  • All context of peer pressure

🟢 Belief

  • And then there is Belief, and how if others believe, it's easier to believe. And they say belief is almost as strong as the will — the will to survive.
  • All these make virtue.
  • Turn this index into an entry form: describe the situation, tag it against Fear / Adamance / Belief
  • Cross-reference each tag with values, time well spent, and what's actually ownable
  • Output: which category dominates, and what that implies about how to respond
  • History table: past entries and how the labeling held up over time

This is meant to sit alongside your coaching notes practice — a structured second opinion, not a replacement for it.
