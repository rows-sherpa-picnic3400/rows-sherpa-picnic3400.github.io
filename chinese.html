Chinese Drills

A coding project for learning Chinese characters through drills and patterns — built to strengthen speaking and reading together, not as separate skills. This page is a placeholder for that build. Below is the working plan.

mock — character drill
你 nǐ 好 hǎo 吗 ma ？

Tap a character to hear it, see its stroke order, and drill it inside a full sentence pattern — not just in isolation.

  • Pattern bank: recurring sentence structures (e.g. 是…的, …了没有?, …的时候) with swappable vocab slots
  • Character drills: stroke order, radicals, and spaced repetition tied to the patterns above, not random flashcards
  • Speaking loop: record yourself against a pattern, compare to a reference reading
  • Reading ladder: short passages built entirely from characters already drilled
  • Progress table: what's solid, what's shaky, what's next — styled like the rest of this site

Once this is built, it'll live at this URL as its own page or a linked project.
